# Privacy Policy for Masidy

**Last Updated: June 11, 2026**

## Overview
Masidy ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the Masidy application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## Information Collection and Use

### Types of Data Collected:

**Personal Data:**
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Chat history and messages
- Usage preferences and settings

**Automatically Collected Data:**
- Device information (OS, device type, model)
- IP address and general location (country/region)
- App usage analytics and crash reports
- Search queries and interactions

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:**
- We use cookies to remember your preferences
- Session tokens for authentication
- Analytics cookies to improve the App

## How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for:
- Providing and maintaining the App functionality
- Personalizing your experience
- Improving and optimizing the App
- Sending service updates and announcements
- Providing customer support
- Analyzing usage patterns
- Ensuring security and preventing fraud

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Data Retention

- **Chat data**: Stored until you delete your account
- **Account data**: Retained while account is active
- **Analytics data**: Retained for 90 days
- **Deleted data**: Permanently removed within 30 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of non-essential tracking

## Third-Party Services

The App uses:
- **Analytics**: To understand usage patterns
- **Cloud Services**: To store and process data securely
- **Authentication Services**: To manage user accounts

These third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
